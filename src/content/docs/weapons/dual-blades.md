---
title: "Dual Blades"
---

The dual blades are a melee weapon that can reflect shots back at attackers.
The reflected shots do 100% of their usual damage, while the wielder takes
only a small portion of damage.

## Overview

| Attribute | Value  |
| --------- | ------ |
| Build     | Medium |
| Type      | Melee  |

## Damage

| Location | Damage         |
| -------- | -------------- |
| Body     | 50x2, 60x2, 70 |
